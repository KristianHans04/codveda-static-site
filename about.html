About Codveda

Our Mission

Codveda Technologies was founded to bridge the gap between academic theory and professional practice. Quality technology education should be accessible, practical, and directly aligned with industry demands.

We build learning experiences around real projects, production-grade tools, and structured mentorship. Our interns do not just learn syntax — they learn to think like engineers, ship working software, and communicate technical decisions clearly.

Every program produces measurable outcomes: deployed applications, clean code, and the confidence to contribute to any engineering team from day one.

Our Journey

Key milestones in Codveda's growth as a technology education provider.

Foundation

Codveda Technologies Established

Founded in Chandrapur, Maharashtra with a mission to deliver practical, industry-aligned IT education to students regardless of geography.

Recognition

Received official AICTE recognition (ID: CORPORATE67458898e8a1a1732610200), validating our curriculum and educational standards at the national level.

Certification

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Achieved ISO quality management certification, demonstrating commitment to consistent quality in education delivery and organizational processes.

Growth

1,800+ Interns Trained

Crossed the milestone of 1,800 happy interns with over 200 receiving stipend scholarships. Expanded to serve 20+ corporate clients.

Expansion

Full Service Portfolio

Expanded offerings to include Web Development, AI/ML, Mobile Development, Data Science, and IT Consultancy services alongside internship programs.

Our Values

The principles that guide how we teach, build, and grow.

Practical First

Every concept is taught through building real, deployable applications. Hands-on experience over passive consumption.

Quality Standards

Clean code, tested software, and thoughtful architecture are non-negotiable. We hold graduates to professional standards.

Community Driven

Learning is stronger in community. We foster collaboration, peer feedback, and knowledge sharing across cohorts.

Globally Accessible

Technology education should not be limited by geography. Our remote-first programs reach learners wherever they are.
